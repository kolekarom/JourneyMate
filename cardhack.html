Places to visit in Pandharpur

Vitthal Mandir

The Vithoba Temple, officially known as Shri Vitthal-Rukmini Mandir is a Hindu temple in Pandharpur, in the Indian state of Maharashtra.

Tulasi Vrundavan Bag

Tulsi Vrindavan in Pundlik Nagar,Pandharpur listed under Parks in Pandharpur.

The Vishnupad Temple

The Vishnupad Temple is one of the most renowned pilgrim & tourist place in Pandharpur in SOlapur district.

ISKCON Pandharpur

ISKCON Pandharpur is a multipurpose hall used for various spiritual, cultural, and social events. It provides a venue for weddings, religious ceremonies. Location: ISKCON Temple Road, Shegaon Dumala, Maharashtra 413304

Gopalpur Mandir

Gopalpur near Pandharpur is a sacred tirtha touched by the lotus feet of Lord Krishna. It lies just 5.4 kms away from the centre of Pandharpur town.
